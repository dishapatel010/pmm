# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Competitive exam aspirants (JEE, NEET, UPSC, GATE, CAT, board exams) preparing under high pressure. They face severe stress, mock test dejection, burnout, peer pressure, and parental expectations. They need an instant, empathetic outlet to dump their thoughts and receive actionable, personalized mental wellness coping mechanisms.

## Product Purpose
Safely act as an empathetic, always-available digital companion that analyzes open-ended journaling logs using Generative AI (Gemini 2.5 Flash), extracts stress triggers and cognitive distortions, and prescribes contextual coping strategies (like breathing exercises) and active motivational support.

## Brand Personality
Comforting, supportive, and empathetic. The companion acts as a warm study partner who understands academic pressure, validates struggles without judgment, and guides the student back to a state of calm.

## Anti-references
- Traditional medical/clinical triage software (which can feel sterile and alarming).
- Standard dry checklist calendars or task trackers.
- Loud, overstimulating, gamified interfaces with bright animations that increase cognitive load.
- Bland, generic "modern SaaS" dashboards that lack empathy.

## Design Principles
1. **Empathy First**: Validate the student's emotions immediately; do not treat them as a "data point".
2. **Low Cognitive Load**: Flat, simple, and clean typography. No flashing animations or complex visual hierarchies.
3. **Calm Interactions**: Visual pacing and clear focal points, using the single-accent Cobalt blue for direct actions.
4. **Actionable Relief**: Provide immediately executable coping steps rather than high-level advice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Maintain strict 4.5:1 text-to-background contrast using the Cobalt Product palette.
- **Motion**: Safe breathing bubble with simple animations. Always honor `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)`.
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Standard focus outlines and logical tab order for simple forms and chat elements.
- **Safety Valve**: Prominent links to national helplines for students experiencing severe clinical distress.
