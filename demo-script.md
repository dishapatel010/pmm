# MindPulse — Demo Script & Entries

Use these three sample entries during presentations to showcase the companion's advanced conversational empathy, cognitive pattern naming, and profile extraction live.

---

### Entry 1: Panicked (Testing Catastrophizing and High Stress)
* **Tone**: Panicked, overwhelmed, catastrophizing.
* **Input Text**:
  > "I just got a 58 percentile on my math JEE mock test today. I've been studying 12 hours a day and my roommate barely studies and gets 98. I am too stupid to clear JEE. I'll never get into an IIT and my parents will be ashamed to look at me."
* **What to watch for**:
  * The companion should reply without clinical labels, but call out the logic loop (e.g. *"You went from a math test score to failing your entire future really fast — let's slow down here"*).
  * Mood signal should map to `Anxious` or `Overwhelmed`, and stress level should be high (e.g., `8` or `9`).
  * Invitation to practice box breathing for 4 minutes.

---

### Entry 2: Neutral (Testing Routine Study and backlog Triggers)
* **Tone**: Tired, stuck, moderate stress.
* **Input Text**:
  > "Finished the daily organic chemistry notes, but I'm still three chapters behind. My revision schedule is completely backed up because of physics mechanics. Feeling pretty tired today, just going to study another hour and sleep."
* **What to watch for**:
  * Triggers list in session close should extract: `backlog` and `revision schedule`.
  * The companion should suggest a short, warm study break or water invitation.
  * Stress level should map around `4` or `5`.

---

### Entry 3: Hopeful (Testing Positive Motivation)
* **Tone**: Determined, optimistic, low stress.
* **Input Text**:
  > "Mock scores went up slightly today in UPSC test series. Still have a long way to go with history syllabus, but I think the active recall cards are starting to pay off. Going to keep this rhythm going tomorrow."
* **What to watch for**:
  * Mood trend should update to `Hopeful` or `Determined`.
  * Stress level maps to `2` or `3` (Calm).
  * The companion should provide active encouragement matching their progress.
